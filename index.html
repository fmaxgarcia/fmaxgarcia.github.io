I am a senior machine learning researcher Roku in the Search and Recommendations team. My research interests are in online learning, reinforcement learning, planning, and optimization. In 2019, I finished my Ph.D. in computer science in the College of Information and Computer Science at the University of Massachusetts - Amherst, advised by Philip Thomas.
